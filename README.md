## Meet Dale

**Dale is a private, on-device AI companion for iPhone.**

He is a grounded, familiar character who remembers the things that matter and keeps the conversation going—without sending your chats to the cloud.

- 🧠 AI inference runs directly on the iPhone
- 📴 Works offline after the one-time model download
- 💭 Conversations and memories stay on the device
- 🔒 No account, ads, analytics, or cloud chat history
- 🍎 Built natively with SwiftUI
